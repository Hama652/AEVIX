# Research Guide

The research directory is reserved for exploratory work that informs future model and systems design.

## Principles

- Capture hypotheses, experiments, and conclusions explicitly.
- Keep reproducible notes separate from throwaway scratch work.
- Move reusable ideas into `src/aevix` once they become infrastructure rather than experimentation.

## Artifact Convention

- Use descriptive experiment names.
- Record configuration snapshots alongside outputs.
- Store large generated artifacts outside version control.
